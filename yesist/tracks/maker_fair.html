About Maker Fair

MakerFair is a unique platform with opportunities for the innovators, makers, and creators to showcase their innovative products to the international community. MakerFair Track 2025 aims to address maximum goals that will aid the world in addressing the difficulties it has faced in recent years, as defined by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. MakerFair Track inspires pre-final, final-year and graduate students in developing prototypes for projects that can be eventually developed into full-fledged products. Young aspirers with enthusiasm and determination to invent and showcase their innovative ideas which are capable from ideation to commercialization can utilize this platform and register for this event.
