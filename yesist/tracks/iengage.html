About IEngage Track

IEngage Track (Industry Engagement) bridges students, professionals and future entrepreneurs with the industry by making them ready for the future. Sharpen your skills, unlock your potential, and witness innovation come alive. This track is all set to take you to the next level of product and startup mastery by providing training on foundational aspects from the Basics level of Cutting-Edge Technologies. IEngage Track has a key focus on Career Development, Industrial Skills Development and Startup/Entrepreneurship Development with our idea “New Age IEngage”.

Vision & Mission

Vision

By partnering with industry experts, YESIST12 unlocks exclusive career development opportunities, in-demand skill development workshops, and entrepreneurial mentorship for participants, empowering them to thrive in their chosen fields.

Mission

Creating pathways for pre-university and university students to collaborate with industry professionals on projects that address critical social challenges. Unleashing the power of social responsibility through community engagement. We empower young minds to become tech-powered changemakers for a brighter tomorrow.

Pillars

Career Building

We empower young professionals to build a strong foundation for their careers by equipping them with in-demand technical skills and allow them to demonstrate with innovations.

Industry Skills Development

Embrace continuous learning and develop industry knowledge to stay relevant and competitive.

Start-up/ Entrepreneurship Development

IEngage provides right guidance with the three stages of startup namely early-stage, venture-funded (growth) stage and late stage. IEngage unlocks your entrepreneurial potential and fuels your confidence for success.
