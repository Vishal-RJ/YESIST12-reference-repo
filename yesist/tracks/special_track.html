About Special Track

Special Track aims at bringing out innovative solutions to solve the issues of mankind, on a global scale. This track was introduced in the 2020 edition of YESIST12 and focuses on offering smarter solutions to some of the most challenging business and humanitarian challenges across the world today. In 2025, Special Track targets providing solutions to elevate the standard of living of every individual by encouraging novel ideas and creative proposals.
